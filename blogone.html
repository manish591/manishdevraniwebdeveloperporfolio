Color Resources

Finding the right color for your website can be hard. Then, you asked 10 different people to tell you the best choice. Out of those 10 different people, 3 or 4 gave you the same advice. Now what do you do? Do you listen to them? Which websites should I use? What about books and tools? That is why we created this blog post - to provide a place where you can find answers to all of these questions. In this article, we will talk about some great resources and tools that will help with choosing the right colors.

Adobe Color

Adobe Color is a web-based color palette generator. Its easy-to-use interface lets you generate beautiful, harmonious color palettes for projects like wedding planning and interior design, as well as for whatever else your creative heart desires. It has quiet nice features, It allows you to generate your custom color pallete, and you can save it for later. You can also follow trends in color pallete.

Coolors

Coolors.co is a color palette generator, built upon the foundations of simplicity and ease-of-use. Here you'll be able to generate color combinations in the form of both palettes and schemes. The Color Wheel allows users to choose a base color and manually adjust the hue, or automatically adjust the hue from an image. Coolors also offers a Schemes section that lets you create custom color schemes directly from any image on the web.

Stock Images

Another important aspect in web design is the images used. Good (or bad) images can make a wonderful website look great or terrible. I use stock images to increase the beauty of my work, but I don’t know any good site where you can download free stock images. Also, most of the sites are really tricky and force you to register just to download one image. That’s why I have decided to make this post about best stock image sources.

Pexels

pexels is great stock image website which provide you with high quality images for free. More than 8000+ photos in their library and they’re adding new photos every day. The key element of taking a photo from online is licenece. With pexels you don't have to worry about licencing freely use these in your design.
